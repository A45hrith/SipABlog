LIST OF SUSTAINABLE SWAPS

Earth is already dying and people think not getting a plastic straw and their Starbucks cup is enough to save it. It’s high time to switch to eco friendly lifestyle to make this world a better place for us as well as our future generations. Eco-friendly products are best because they reduce the carbon footprint and they are biodegradable.

Bamboo Tooth Brush

Plastic tooth brush accounts for the global plastic waste problem and it is non biodegradable and unsustainable. Every year billions of plastic tooth brushes are thrown away , they finally end up in Oceans or landfills and cause immense pollution. Advantages of bamboo tooth brush :
1. Eco friendly and organic
2. Sustainable and renewable

Shop here-: SustainKart - Bamboo Tooth Brush


Menstrual Cups

Usage of tampons and sanitary pads made up of plastics are extremely harmful and toxic to environment. As they are non biodegradable, they end up in oceans and landfills making water and soil toxic ,which indirectly effects the aquatic life also . Using one menstrual cup can replace the usage of tampons and pads for 4-5years , reducing the waste byproduct’s .

Reusable Water Bottles

Annually Indians buy billions of plastic water/beverage bottles contributing to major percentage of non biodegradable waste. The alternatives are : 1. Insulated stainless steel water bottles 2. Copper water bottles 3. Glass water bottles 4. Aluminium water bottle

Chewable Toothpaste Tablets

These will cut down the amount of plastic which is being used to make tooth paste tubes and they are also easy to ship , which accounts for lesser carbon foot print. Chewable tooth paste tablets have the same tooth paste formula but they are made without water. They are easy to use and sustainable. Just chew these tablets and brush your teeth with ordinary tooth brush and you are good to go.
