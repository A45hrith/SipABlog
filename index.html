Black Fungal disease

Black Fungus, also called as mucormycosis is a fungal infectious disease. It's a rare but fatal infection. The infection is presented as fungus growing mostly in and around blood vessels, sinuses, oral activity, brain or lungs. It can also infect other parts of body too.
